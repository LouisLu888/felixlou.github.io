Learn, practice and teach

1.Understanding yourself

![](/images/wechat/10_steps_to_a_successful_career/17813965490240.23088923255014004.jpg) Career growth is more organic when you are working for a company which has the same value as yours. When changing jobs, you need to look at the company and manager’s values; always remember  you are hiring a compnay as well as the company is hiring you.

Leaders must have the ability to excite people, simiplify situations and make priority calls as well as calm people down.

Turn best practices into habits. This wil simplify your life and make you a more predictable and easy to follow leader. Once a pratice becomes your habit, it is part of your muscle memory, you automatically do it without thinking. It is hard to forgot and ignore, just like it is hard to unlearn how to ride a bike.

Your job is to make your boss as successful as possible.

Prepare 1-5 questions for your next meeting; Ask at least 1-2 of them. Write down the best questions your heard from the meeting, prepare to use them in the next meeting.

2.Elevating your position

![](/images/wechat/10_steps_to_a_successful_career/17813965490930.7890944811526557.jpg)

Looking one level up means using my manager’s priorities as my own and working on the most important tasks for my group, not just my own assignments. That is easier said than done.

Use your strengths to compensate for your managers’ weakness, give him credit and make your manager successful.

Seeing problems, thinking through solutions and solving problems at higher level certainly make you an obvious succession candidate.

Every position comes with four basic attributes:

responsibility visibility resources reward

Observe successful leaders within your reach can help you even more. Write down what you observe and internalize the principles and sound business practices while your memory is still fresh.

The super glue producer is the leader of the group and will most likely be the candidate for the next promostion.

Sharing infomation is as important is as sharing success, especially well-educated information and opinions. People around you will treat you as the center of the infomation, solutions and source of help.

Three kinds of knowledge: You aware of and you know how to apply it; You aware of but not interested, no capability to acquire it; You are not aware of, u don’t know they are exist. We make decision not to pursue the 2nd body of the knowledge when we become aware of our interest and limitations. We have to be secure and open-mind to uncover the 3rd body of knowledge.

What are u trying to tell me? What do u want me to do? How do u want me to feel?

To managers, repecting their time and feelings while providing suggested solutions with problems presneted.

Exercise for great health, work hard, demostrate your skills and strengths, follow a great leader and you will be successful.

Managing up is about managing expectations from the corner office and prociding visibility and information to your managers, keep everyone updated with you and your team’s latest accomplishments, risk and solutions. You are also your manager’s internal marketing agent to get all the good news out and spin the best news into chalenges. You are his external marketing representations to get speaking engagements, award nominnations and take advantages of broad member or advisor opportunities.

Risk and solutions are highly entertaining. It is your best opportunity to shine. Risk attracts attention throughtout the org; the person who with clever win-win solutions is the hero who save the day.

A people hire A+ people, B people hire C or D levels of people.

3.Setting your goals

![](/images/wechat/10_steps_to_a_successful_career/17813965491470.27102929725760827.jpg)

4LS: To live to my fullset potential; To love the people around me; To learn and mentor everyday; To leave a legacy — help others reach their fullest potential

3S: Survival, Success, Significance

Many people set their goals too slow — only at survival level. eg: pay off mortgage; save money for childrens’ college fund; survive layoffs

Significant goals can elevate us from our own interests to group and community interests.

I help you because I believe that you can be more successful with my help. I will not live that long to see your success but it will become my legacy whether I see it or not.
